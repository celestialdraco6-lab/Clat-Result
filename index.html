Welcome to CLAT 2026

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is a national level admission test for admissions to undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) law programmes offered by National Law Universities in India.

CLAT is organized by the Consortium of National Law Universities comprising representative universities. All admissions to the 5-year integrated LL.B. and LL.M. programmes commencing in the Academic Year 2026–27 shall be through CLAT 2026.
