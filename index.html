Undergraduate Programme (UG)

Admission to the five-year integrated undergraduate law programmes offered by participating National Law Universities is conducted through CLAT 2026.

  • 10+2 or equivalent examination
  • Minimum qualifying marks as prescribed
  • No upper age limit

Postgraduate Programme (PG)

Admission to the one-year LL.M. programmes of National Law Universities is through CLAT PG 2026.

  • LL.B. degree or equivalent
  • Minimum qualifying marks as prescribed
  • Indian and eligible foreign nationals
